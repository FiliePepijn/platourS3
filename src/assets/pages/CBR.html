SCRUM

For our scrum board we use notion to keep track of our tasks. In the beginning of our project, we didn’t keep track of what sprint we were and what we should have done in each sprint. I asked dirk what the best way is to set tasks for a sprint and decide on what we as a group should do in a sprint. Dirk told me to divide all the tasks and use the double diamond with discover define develop deliver

So, I divided every aspect of the double diamond into the weeks of our sprints (sprint of 2 weeks). For every sprint I make a sprint planning where I get tasks from the backlog and put them in our to do list. Then in the first sprint stand-up we assign the tasks.

Meetings

During the scrum period we did serveral retrospectives where we added the things we would like to keep add have less of and have more off, then we put them up on a board and discuss the cards. This was a fast and effective way to get an outcome.

Reflect

At the end of the project, I as scrum master began to let some things slip regarding scrum planning, so we sat together to make a new planning from then on to get things back on track.
