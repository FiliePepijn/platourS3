Trend Analysis

With the sports category in mind, I started doing research on current problems in sports or things that can help an athlete achieve better results. I used trend analysis for this project to find upcoming technologies that are potentially happening in the future of sports. By looking at upcoming advancements I was able to find the integration of AI and holo tech as an upcoming trend. So, then I could use these studies in our own project, such as keeping athlete motivation and improving training efficiency. Trend analysis provided the base for a concept that is not only futuristic but also grounded in the potential of current and upcoming technological trends.

Study 1

Study 1 shows that they made footballers take penalties with VR-glasses. A camera can see your shot and instantly help you aim in the right direction. This resulted in a 35 increase of scoring possibility.

Study 2

Omega’s Swiss timing division is developing advance AI and computer vision systems to track and analyse Olympic athletes’ performances in real-time at the 2024 Paris Olympics, this includes motion sensors on athlete's clothing, 3D body imaging, and algorithms tailored to each sport to provide detailed metrics and insights beyond just start and finish times.

Study 3

This study gets into AI & VR how they are helping athletes reach their best performance. AI takes care of the data side, creating personalised training plans by analysing strengths and weaknesses, The VR part makes practice more real with simulations. Together, they offer a great combo for improving your own records and technique. They also researched the best way to combine these two technologies to make it work more smoothly.

Creating the concept

For the concept we made a running track, with a hologram projector, and an athelete that is running against a hologram. The hologram is a representation of the athelete's best time. so the athelete can see if he is running faster or slower than his best time.
